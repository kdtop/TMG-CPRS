Changing Views on the Consults tab

Changing the view of the Consults tab allows you to focus the list of consults on one of several criteria. Focusing the list will speed up the selection process.

You may change the Consults view to only include the following problems:

·     All Consults

·     Consults by Status

·     Consults by Service

·     Consults by Date Range

 

To change the view, select View on the menu and select the desired list items.

You may select the Custom list option on the menu to further focus the list of notes you wish to have displayed. From the List Selected Consults dialog, you may choose to display consults by any combination of Service, Status, and date range.
