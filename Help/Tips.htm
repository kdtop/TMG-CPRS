TIPS & TRICKS & Shortcuts

Here are a few tips that might help you use CPRS better.

·      In most windows or boxes, you can click on your right mouse button to get a pop-up window with a definition and/or a list of possible actions.

·      In the sign-on box, you must press the tab key (not Enter or Return) to move between the access and verify code fields.

Shortcut: In the Access Code box, type your access code, then a semicolon, then your verify code, then hit the Enter or Return key, and you will go immediately to the Patient Selection window.

 

·      You can see detailed information about items on the Cover Sheet by double-clicking on them.
